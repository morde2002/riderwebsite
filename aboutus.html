Our Company

At Texol Energies Uganda, we are not just a company; we are a beacon of excellence in the oil and gas industry. Since our inception in 2022, we have been redefining the standards of oil and gas marketing with a strong presence boasting over 10 stations in and around Kampala. Our journey has been driven by a passionate and experienced management team, committed to delivering the highest quality products and services to our valued customers. Our Managing Director's unwavering dedication to excellent customer service has been the driving force behind our success.
